On the **Profile** section, you can view your profile information: name, email, and user ID. You can also review your user rights and change the application appearance (light or dark mode).

Additionally, you can configure your notification preferences. Please keep in mind that these are **enabled by default**. 

There are two types of notifications (received via email):
* **Online notifications**: real-time alerts delivered while you are using the platform
* **Digest notifications**: daily summaries containing updates on the organizations you participate

These notifications help the users stay up to date with each other’s activity.
