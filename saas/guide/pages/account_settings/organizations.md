In the **Organizations** section, you can view the organizations you are part of and the corresponding roles you have in each of them.

![image](../../../screenshots/account_settings_organizations.png)

In this section, you can also set notification preferences for the organizations you belong to. To do this, click the bell icon in the relevant organization entry and choose your preferred notification setting.

![image](../../../screenshots/account_settings_organizations_notifications.png)
