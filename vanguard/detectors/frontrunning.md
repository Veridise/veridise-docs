## Summary and Usage

The Frontrunning detector determines which functions of a smart contract are
susceptible to frontrunning attacks based on the storage variable accesses.

### Usage

The Frontrunning detector is invoked by selecting "Frontrunning" in the Detector
selection during the tool configuration step.
